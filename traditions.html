Traditions & Festivals

Major Holidays

Christmas: Celebrated with nativity scenes and festive meals.
Easter: Marked by religious processions and traditional foods.

Regional Festivals

Carnival of Venice: Famous for its elaborate masks and costumes.
Palio di Siena: A historic horse race held twice a year in Siena.
