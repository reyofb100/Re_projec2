About Italy

History

Italy has a long and storied history, from the ancient Roman Empire to the Renaissance period that shaped much of modern Western culture.

Geography

Located in Southern Europe, Italy is known for its beautiful landscapes, including the Alps in the north and the Mediterranean coastlines.

Regions of Italy

Italy is divided into 20 regions, each with its unique traditions, dialects, and culinary specialties.
